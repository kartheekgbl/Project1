Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one my favorite food. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt buuter in a frying part over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl!
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a special fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still misize remove the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
